What is Yigtha?

Yigtha is an eldritch being, an occupant of the deep Void. She is virtually unknown to the inhabitants of Aedrexus, her very existence a matter of the furthest esoterica. She has graced one unfortunate elf with her blessing, rendering him immortal and almighty in her image. Her dark intentions remain a mystery.

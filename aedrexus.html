Aedrexus

The Realm of Aedrexus is a worldbuilding project between my husband and I. Currently it is a largely modified version of Forgotten Realms, but we're slowly building onto it, with an ideal plan of everything being original or heavily modified eventually.

The setting can be described as dark medieval high fantasy with cosmic horror and general horror elements.

Characters

  • Ambrose, Yigtha's Chosen
  • Alexios Seljeva, Primarch (belongs to my husband)
  • Crania Umbra, Female Manipulator
  • Catald Paraskeva, Doubly Cursed
  • Gorog Thothgor, Bitch
